<h1>IC Rules</h1>
<p>1.1) This is not a TDM server, this is a roleplay server. Combat is allowed but only under certain conditions.</p>

<p>1.2) Roleplay a believable character for the setting. Avoid pop culture references in IC in any form and do not break immersion. Be aware of the server lore.</p>

<p>1.3) In order to engage another player in combat, an immediate scene of roleplay must precede the desired engagement. There must be at least an emote preceding any combat interaction in order to signify to the other player your intent to switch from roleplay to mechanics.</p>

<hr>

<h1>OOC Rules</h1>
<p>2.1) Do not play on this server if you are underage.</p>

<p>2.2) Try to act in good faith.</p>

<p>2.3) Do not IC in OOC. Do not OOC in IC.</p>

<p>2.4) Do not use slurs both OOC and IC.</p>

<p>2.5) Do not harass fellow community members.</p>

<p>2.6) Do not intentionally ruin the experience of others.</p>

<p>2.7) Do not metagame for the purpose of ruining someone's experience.</p>

<p>2.8) Don't use any sort of explosives without explicit permission from staff.</p>

<p>2.9) A staff ruling for a ticket is valid for the given situation. Do not attempt to bait other staff to overrule their decision. If you believe the ruling to be unfair, make a staff complaint.
Do not intentionally break the preferences of the other party.</p>

<hr>

<h1>Mature Content</h1>
<p>3.1) ERP is allowed and encouraged, however do not roleplay anything that would break Discord ToS.</p>

<p>3.2) Do not interrupt an ERP scene without a robust IC motive.</p>

<p>3.3) Do not break the preferences of the other party. Communicate extreme fetishes through OOC means (LOOC, Discord etc.)</p>
